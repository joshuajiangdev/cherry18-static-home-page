Flow

Flow is the first privacy focused mobile expenses tracking App help you track your spending with split payment function and Venmo support. All your data will be stored on your phone.

Features

Build with privacy in mind

We know how it feels when others are collecting your spending histories. That is why we build Flow with privacy as our first priority.

Split your expenses when you pay for others

Hanging out with others and feel hard to track your expenses when you pay for others? Now you can track how much you paid and others should pay you back.

Detailed Report

Want to know where to cut your expenses? We have a detailed report with charts ready for you! You can view it from daily, monthly or even yearly basis.

Customizable category system

Everyone has their own spending pattern, so you can create your own categories and sub-categories to better suit your needs.
